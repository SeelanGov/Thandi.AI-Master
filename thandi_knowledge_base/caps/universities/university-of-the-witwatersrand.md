## Overview
Wits recognizes CAPS NSC using APS (Admission Point Score) calculations, with specific subject requirements for different faculties.

## CAPS Admission Requirements
APS calculation: Best 6 subjects including English and Mathematics (where required)
Minimum endorsement: Bachelor's degree pass
Language requirements: English Home Language or First Additional Language minimum Level 4
Additional tests: Some programs require additional assessments

## Faculty Requirements

### Engineering and Built Environment
Minimum APS: 40-42 points
Mathematics: Level 6-7 (70-100%)
Physical Sciences: Level 6 (70-79%)
English: Level 4 (50-59%)
Additional: Technical drawing beneficial for some programs

### Commerce, Law and Management
Minimum APS: 38-42 points
Mathematics: Level 5-6 (60-79%) for most programs
English: Level 4-5 (50-69%)
Additional: Some programs require specific subjects like Accounting

### Health Sciences
Minimum APS: 42-45 points
Mathematics: Level 6 (70-79%)
Physical Sciences: Level 6 (70-79%)
Life Sciences: Level 6 (70-79%)
English: Level 5 (60-69%)

### Humanities
Minimum APS: 35-40 points
English: Level 4-5 (50-69%)
Mathematics: Level 4+ where required
Additional: Some programs require specific humanities subjects

### Science
Minimum APS: 38-42 points
Mathematics: Level 5-6 (60-79%)
Physical Sciences: Level 5-6 (60-79%) for most programs
English: Level 4 (50-59%)

## Program Examples
BEng Mechanical Engineering: APS 42+, Math Level 7, Physical Sciences Level 6, English Level 4
BCom Accounting: APS 40+, Math Level 6, English Level 4, Accounting beneficial
BSc Computer Science: APS 40+, Math Level 6, Physical Sciences Level 5, English Level 4
MBChB Medicine: APS 45+, Math Level 6, Physical Sciences Level 6, Life Sciences Level 6

## CAPS Specific Notes
No bonus points for Advanced Programme subjects (CAPS doesn't offer AP)
Standard APS calculation applies to all CAPS students
Some bridging programs available for students not meeting direct entry requirements
Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) options available for mature students
