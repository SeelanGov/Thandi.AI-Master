## Overview
University of Johannesburg recognizes CAPS NSC using APS (Admission Point Score) calculations, with comprehensive support for diverse student backgrounds.

## CAPS Admission Requirements
APS calculation: Best 6 subjects including English and Mathematics (where required)
Minimum endorsement: Bachelor's degree pass
Language requirements: English proficiency required
Additional tests: Some programs require additional selection criteria

## Faculty Requirements

### Engineering and Built Environment
Minimum APS: 35-40 points
Mathematics: Level 6-7 (70-100%)
Physical Sciences: Level 6 (70-79%)
English: Level 4 (50-59%)
Additional: Extended programs available for lower APS scores

### Science
Minimum APS: 32-38 points
Mathematics: Level 5-6 (60-79%)
Physical Sciences: Level 5-6 (60-79%) for most programs
Life Sciences: Level 5-6 (60-79%) for biological programs
English: Level 4 (50-59%)

### Health Sciences
Minimum APS: 38-42 points
Mathematics: Level 6 (70-79%)
Physical Sciences: Level 6 (70-79%)
Life Sciences: Level 6 (70-79%)
English: Level 5 (60-69%)

### Economic and Financial Sciences
Minimum APS: 30-35 points
Mathematics: Level 4-5 (50-69%) for most programs
English: Level 4 (50-59%)
Additional: Mathematical Literacy accepted for many programs

### Humanities
Minimum APS: 28-33 points
English: Level 4 (50-59%)
Mathematics: Level 4+ where required
Additional: Mathematical Literacy widely accepted

### Education
Minimum APS: 28-32 points
English: Level 4 (50-59%)
Mathematics: Level 4+ for Mathematics teaching
Additional: Mathematical Literacy accepted for many teaching specializations

## Program Examples
BEng Electrical Engineering: APS 38+, Math Level 6, Physical Sciences Level 6, English Level 4
BCom Accounting: APS 33+, Math Level 5, English Level 4
BSc Computer Science: APS 35+, Math Level 6, Physical Sciences Level 5, English Level 4
BEd Foundation Phase: APS 30+, English Level 4, Mathematical Literacy accepted

## CAPS Student Support
Extensive foundation and extended programs
Academic Development Programs (ADP) for students needing additional support
Bridging courses in Mathematics and Physical Sciences
Comprehensive student support services
Financial aid specifically targeted at CAPS students

## Accessibility Features
Lower APS requirements compared to other major universities
Mathematical Literacy accepted for many programs
Multiple entry pathways including Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)
Strong focus on transformation and inclusive education
Comprehensive academic support throughout studies
