## Overview
University of Pretoria recognizes CAPS NSC using APS (Admission Point Score) calculations, with specific requirements for different faculties and programs.

## CAPS Admission Requirements
APS calculation: Best 6 subjects including English and Mathematics (where required)
Minimum endorsement: Bachelor's degree pass
Language requirements: English proficiency required, Afrikaans beneficial for some programs
Additional tests: Some programs require additional selection processes

## Faculty Requirements

### Engineering, Built Environment and Information Technology
Minimum APS: 38-42 points
Mathematics: Level 6-7 (70-100%)
Physical Sciences: Level 6 (70-79%)
English: Level 4-5 (50-69%)
Additional: Some IT programs may accept Mathematical Literacy

### Economic and Management Sciences
Minimum APS: 35-40 points
Mathematics: Level 5-6 (60-79%) for most programs
English: Level 4-5 (50-69%)
Additional: Mathematical Literacy accepted for some programs

### Health Sciences
Minimum APS: 40-45 points
Mathematics: Level 6 (70-79%)
Physical Sciences: Level 6 (70-79%)
Life Sciences: Level 6 (70-79%)
English: Level 5 (60-69%)

### Humanities
Minimum APS: 32-38 points
English: Level 4-5 (50-69%)
Mathematics: Level 4+ where required
Additional: Mathematical Literacy accepted for many programs

### Natural and Agricultural Sciences
Minimum APS: 35-40 points
Mathematics: Level 5-6 (60-79%)
Physical Sciences: Level 5-6 (60-79%) for most programs
Life Sciences: Level 5-6 (60-79%) for biological programs
English: Level 4-5 (50-69%)

### Veterinary Science
Minimum APS: 42+ points
Mathematics: Level 6 (70-79%)
Physical Sciences: Level 6 (70-79%)
Life Sciences: Level 6 (70-79%)
English: Level 5 (60-69%)

## Program Examples
BEng Computer Engineering: APS 40+, Math Level 6, Physical Sciences Level 6, English Level 4
BCom Accounting: APS 38+, Math Level 5, English Level 4
BSc Computer Science: APS 38+, Math Level 6, Physical Sciences Level 5, English Level 4
BVSc Veterinary Science: APS 42+, Math Level 6, Physical Sciences Level 6, Life Sciences Level 6

## CAPS Specific Features
Flexible admission policies with Mathematical Literacy accepted for many programs
Strong support systems for CAPS students
Extended programs available for students needing additional preparation
Recognition of diverse educational backgrounds

## Additional Opportunities
Foundation programs for students not meeting direct entry requirements
Bridging courses in Mathematics and Physical Sciences
Career guidance and academic support services
Financial aid and bursary opportunities specifically for CAPS students
