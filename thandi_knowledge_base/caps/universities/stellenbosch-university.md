## Overview
Stellenbosch University recognizes CAPS NSC using APS (Admission Point Score) calculations, with language requirements in Afrikaans and English.

## CAPS Admission Requirements
APS calculation: Best 6 subjects including English and/or Afrikaans
Minimum endorsement: Bachelor's degree pass
Language requirements: Afrikaans and/or English proficiency required depending on program
Additional tests: Some programs require additional assessments or portfolios

## Faculty Requirements

### Engineering
Minimum APS: 42-45 points
Mathematics: Level 6-7 (70-100%)
Physical Sciences: Level 6-7 (70-100%)
English/Afrikaans: Level 4-5 (50-69%)
Additional: Some programs taught in Afrikaans

### Economic and Management Sciences
Minimum APS: 40-43 points
Mathematics: Level 5-6 (60-79%)
English/Afrikaans: Level 4-5 (50-69%)
Additional: Accounting beneficial for some programs

### Medicine and Health Sciences
Minimum APS: 45+ points
Mathematics: Level 6-7 (70-100%)
Physical Sciences: Level 6-7 (70-100%)
Life Sciences: Level 6-7 (70-100%)
English/Afrikaans: Level 5-6 (60-79%)

### Arts and Social Sciences
Minimum APS: 35-40 points
English/Afrikaans: Level 4-5 (50-69%)
Mathematics: Level 4+ where required
Additional: Some programs have specific subject requirements

### Science
Minimum APS: 38-42 points
Mathematics: Level 5-6 (60-79%)
Physical Sciences: Level 5-6 (60-79%) for most programs
English/Afrikaans: Level 4-5 (50-69%)

## Program Examples
BEng Civil Engineering: APS 44+, Math Level 7, Physical Sciences Level 6, English/Afrikaans Level 4
BCom Accounting: APS 42+, Math Level 6, English/Afrikaans Level 4, Accounting beneficial
BSc Computer Science: APS 40+, Math Level 6, Physical Sciences Level 5, English/Afrikaans Level 4
MBChB Medicine: APS 45+, Math Level 6, Physical Sciences Level 6, Life Sciences Level 6

## Language Considerations
Many programs offered in Afrikaans with English support
Some programs fully bilingual or English-medium
Language proficiency may affect admission for certain programs
Support available for students needing language assistance

## CAPS Specific Notes
Standard APS calculation for all CAPS students
No Advanced Programme considerations (CAPS doesn't offer AP)
Strong emphasis on Mathematics and Physical Sciences for STEM programs
Bridging programs available for students needing additional preparation
