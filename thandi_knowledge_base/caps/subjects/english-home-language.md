## Overview
CAPS English Home Language develops advanced language skills including literature analysis, creative writing, and critical thinking. Required for university admission and essential for academic success.

## CAPS Requirements
Structured progression: Grade 10 foundations (language skills, basic literature), Grade 11 advanced concepts (complex texts, essay writing), Grade 12 integration and applications (advanced literature, research skills). Assessment: 25% SBA (oral presentations, creative writing, research tasks), 75% final examination (Paper 1: Language in Context, Paper 2: Literature, Paper 3: Creative Writing).

## Career Pathways
English Home Language is beneficial for:
- Law and Legal Services
- Journalism and Media
- Teaching and Education
- Marketing and Communications
- Public Relations and Advertising
- Publishing and Editing
- Translation and Interpretation
- Government and Public Service

## University Requirements
Required for most university programs:
- Minimum Level 4 (50-59%) for most programs
- Level 5-6 (60-79%) for competitive programs like Law, Medicine
- Level 6-7 (70-100%) for English Literature and Language programs
- Essential for academic writing and research skills

## APS Calculations
English contributes to APS calculations for all programs. Strong English skills are crucial for academic success across all fields of study.

## Academic Foundation
Develops critical thinking, analytical writing, and communication skills essential for university success. Strong English performance indicates readiness for higher education academic demands.
