## Overview
CAPS Computer Applications Technology (CAT) covers practical computer skills including software applications, programming basics, and digital literacy. Focuses on real-world technology applications.

## CAPS Requirements
Structured progression: Grade 10 foundations (basic computer skills, software applications), Grade 11 advanced concepts (databases, web design, programming basics), Grade 12 integration and applications (advanced projects, system design). Assessment: 25% SBA (practical projects, assignments, portfolio), 75% final examination (practical and theoretical components).

## Career Pathways
CAT is beneficial for:
- Information Technology and Software Development
- Web Design and Digital Media
- Database Administration
- Business Analysis and Systems Design
- Digital Marketing and E-commerce
- Computer Training and Support
- Teaching (Computer Studies and IT)
- Office Administration and Management

## University Requirements
Useful for various IT and business programs:
- Information Technology: Minimum 60-65% (Level 5)
- Computer Science: Minimum 60-70% (Level 5-6)
- Business Information Systems: Minimum 55-65% (Level 4-5)
- Digital Media: Minimum 55-60% (Level 4-5)

## APS Calculations
CAT contributes to APS calculations for IT, business, and media programs. Demonstrates practical technology skills and digital literacy.

## Practical Skills
Develops hands-on experience with business software, databases, web technologies, and basic programming. Excellent preparation for technology-related careers and modern workplace requirements.
