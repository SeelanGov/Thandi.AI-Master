## Overview
CAPS Business Studies covers business environments, business ventures, business roles, and business operations. Provides foundation for entrepreneurship, management, and commerce careers.

## CAPS Requirements
Structured progression: Grade 10 foundations (business environments, entrepreneurship basics), Grade 11 advanced concepts (business functions, marketing, human resources), Grade 12 integration and applications (business strategy, investment, business plan development). Assessment: 25% SBA (tests, assignments, business plan projects), 75% final examination (case studies and theoretical applications).

## Career Pathways
Business Studies is beneficial for:
- Business Management and Administration
- Entrepreneurship and Small Business Development
- Marketing and Sales Management
- Human Resources Management
- Project Management
- Retail and Supply Chain Management
- Teaching (Business Studies and Economics)
- Public Administration

## University Requirements
Useful for various commerce and management programs:
- Business Management: Minimum 50-60% (Level 4-5)
- Marketing: Minimum 50-60% (Level 4-5)
- Human Resources: Minimum 50-55% (Level 4)
- Public Administration: Minimum 50-55% (Level 4)

## APS Calculations
Business Studies contributes to APS calculations for commerce and management programs. Demonstrates understanding of business principles and entrepreneurial thinking.

## Practical Applications
Develops practical business skills including business plan writing, market analysis, financial planning, and strategic thinking. Excellent preparation for students planning to start their own businesses or pursue management careers.
