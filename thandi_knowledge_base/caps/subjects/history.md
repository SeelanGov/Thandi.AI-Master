## Overview
CAPS History covers South African history, world history, and historical skills including source analysis, research, and critical thinking about the past.

## CAPS Requirements
Structured progression: Grade 10 foundations (early South African history, world civilizations), Grade 11 advanced concepts (apartheid, world wars, decolonization), Grade 12 integration and applications (contemporary history, historical research). Assessment: 25% SBA (research projects, source-based tasks, essays), 75% final examination (Paper 1: Source-based questions, Paper 2: Essay questions).

## Career Pathways
History is beneficial for:
- Law and Legal Services
- Journalism and Media
- Teaching and Education
- Museum and Heritage Management
- Government and Public Service
- Research and Academia
- Tourism and Cultural Industries
- International Relations

## University Requirements
Useful for various programs:
- Law: Minimum 60-65% (Level 5)
- Humanities: Minimum 50-60% (Level 4-5)
- Social Sciences: Minimum 55-65% (Level 4-5)
- Education: Minimum 50-55% (Level 4)

## APS Calculations
History contributes to APS calculations for humanities, social sciences, and law programs. Demonstrates critical thinking and analytical writing skills.

## Skills Development
Develops critical analysis, research skills, essay writing, and understanding of cause and effect. Excellent preparation for careers requiring analytical thinking and communication skills.
