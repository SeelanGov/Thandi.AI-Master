## Overview
CAPS Life Orientation covers personal development, citizenship education, health promotion, and career guidance. Compulsory subject that does not count toward APS calculations.

## CAPS Requirements
Structured progression: Grade 10 foundations (personal development, health basics), Grade 11 advanced concepts (career exploration, citizenship), Grade 12 integration and applications (life skills, career planning). Assessment: 100% SBA (projects, presentations, practical tasks, portfolio development). No final examination.

## Career Pathways
Life Orientation provides foundation skills for:
- All career paths (life skills development)
- Social Work and Community Development
- Health Promotion and Wellness
- Teaching and Education
- Human Resources and Training
- Sports and Recreation Management
- Counseling and Psychology

## University Requirements
Does not count toward APS calculations at any South African university
Provides essential life skills and career awareness
Required for NSC completion but excluded from admission calculations

## APS Calculations
Life Orientation has 0.0 APS weight and is excluded from all university admission calculations. Students must pass Life Orientation to receive their NSC but it does not affect university admission scores.

## Skills Development
Develops personal management, career awareness, health consciousness, and citizenship skills. Essential for personal development and life preparation but not for academic progression.
