## Overview
CAPS Mathematics covers algebra, functions, calculus, geometry, trigonometry, statistics, and probability. Essential for STEM careers and university admission to science, engineering, and mathematics programs.

## CAPS Requirements
Structured progression: Grade 10 foundations, Grade 11 advanced concepts, Grade 12 integration and applications. Assessment: 25% SBA (tests, assignments, investigations), 75% final examination (Paper 1: algebra/functions, Paper 2: geometry/trigonometry/statistics).

## Career Pathways
Mathematics is required for:
- All engineering disciplines
- Medicine and health sciences
- Pure sciences (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics)
- Computer Science and Information Technology
- Actuarial Science and Statistics
- Architecture and Quantity Surveying
- Teaching (Mathematics and Science subjects)

## University Requirements
Most universities require Mathematics (not Mathematical Literacy) for STEM programs:
- Engineering: Minimum 60-70% (Level 5-6)
- Medicine: Minimum 70-80% (Level 6-7)
- Science degrees: Minimum 60-70% (Level 5-6)
- Commerce (some programs): Minimum 50-60% (Level 4-5)

## APS Calculations
Mathematics contributes to Admission Point Score (APS) calculations at all South African universities. Higher mathematics marks significantly improve university admission chances and program options.

## Mathematical Literacy Alternative
Students struggling with Mathematics may consider Mathematical Literacy, but this severely limits university program options, excluding all STEM fields and most commerce programs.
