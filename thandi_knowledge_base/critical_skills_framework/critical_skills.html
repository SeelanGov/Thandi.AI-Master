What Are Critical and Scarce Skills?

These are high-demand jobs prioritized by government to grow the economy. Choosing these careers:

  • Boosts your job prospects
  • Aligns with PYEI training programs
  • Qualifies you for SETA learnerships

Pro Tip: Critical skills = faster employment + better funding options.
