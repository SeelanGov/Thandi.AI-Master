# TVET College Pathways Knowledge Base

This module contains information about South Africa's TVET (Technical and Vocational Education and Training) colleges, enabling Thandi to answer questions like:
- "I didn't get into university, what are my options?"
- "Where can I study to become an electrician?"
- "What TVET colleges are in my area?"
- "Can I go to university after TVET?"

## Why TVET Matters

TVET colleges are critical for:
- Students who don't meet university requirements
- Those seeking practical, hands-on training
- Quick entry into the workforce (1-3 years)
- Artisan and technical career paths
- More affordable education options

## Structure

- `tvet_colleges.json` - Main data file with all TVET college information
- `CONTENT-SPEC.md` - Detailed specification for content structure
- Upload script will be created similar to university pathways

## Program Types

### NC(V) - National Certificate Vocational
- Levels 2-4 (NQF 2-4)
- 3-year program
- Theory + practical
- Direct employment or N4-N6 progression

### Report 191 (N1-N6)
- Engineering Studies (N1-N6)
- Business Studies (N4-N6)
- Modular system
- Leads to National Diploma

### Occupational Certificates
- Artisan trades
- Apprenticeships
- Trade tests
- Industry-recognized qualifications
