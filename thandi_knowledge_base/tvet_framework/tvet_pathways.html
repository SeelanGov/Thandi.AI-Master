What Are TVET Colleges?

TVET (Technical and Vocational Education and Training) colleges provide hands-on training for careers in engineering, business, agriculture, and more. They’re affordable and focus on real-world skills.

Pro Tip: TVET leads to quick employment or university bridging.

TVET Programs

Main options:

ProgramDurationCareers
NCV (National Certificate Vocational) No Matric, 3 years Electrician, Hospitality Manager
NATED Diplomas (N1–N6) With Matric, 18–24 months Mechanical Engineer, Bookkeeper

Entry Requirements

  • NCV: Grade 9 or higher (some accept Grade 11).
  • NATED: Matric (NQF 4) with subjects like Maths for Engineering.
