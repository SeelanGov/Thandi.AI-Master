What Are Bursaries and Scholarships?

  • Bursary: Covers tuition, accommodation, or books. Often requires a work-back agreement (e.g., teach after graduation).
  • Scholarship: Rewards academic, sport, or leadership excellence. Usually no repayment needed.

Pro Tip: Apply to both — bursaries for need, scholarships for merit.

Eligibility

  • Academic: Most require strong Matric results (e.g., 60%+ for NSFAS, 70%+ for scholarships).
  • Financial Need: Bursaries like NSFAS require proof of household income.
  • Citizenship: Most require South African citizenship or permanent residency.
