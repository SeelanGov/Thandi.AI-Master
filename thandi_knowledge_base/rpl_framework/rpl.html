Have work experience but no formal qualifications? EduEasy and Thandi help you validate your skills through Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) to earn NQF qualifications — all in one place.

What Is RPL?

RPL recognizes your work experience, informal training, or life skills as credits toward a formal qualification (NQF Levels 4–7). It’s perfect for adult learners or workers seeking career advancement.

Benefit: Skip full courses and fast-track your qualification.

RPL Examples

How RPL works in different sectors:

SectorExampleNQF LevelProvider
Construction Experienced builder earning NQF 4 NQF 4 CETA
Customer Service Call centre worker earning NQF 4 NQF 4 Services SETA
Management Team leader earning NQF 5 NQF 5 SAQA

Eligibility

  • Experience: At least 3–5 years of relevant work or informal learning.
  • Documents: Provide work records, references, or portfolios.
