## Overview
Includes business environments, operations, management, entrepreneurship, and legislation.

## IEB Requirements
No prereq; SBA 25% (presentations, cases); exams 75% (two papers). Focuses on contemporary issues.

## CAPS Comparison
Aligned topics (Gr10 environments, Gr11 functions, Gr12 strategies); IEB more entrepreneurial.

## University Notes
For BCom paths; min 4 (50-59%) (e.g., UJ BCom: 4+, Wits: 5+, SU: 50%).
