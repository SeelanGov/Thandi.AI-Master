## Overview
Covers biodiversity, life processes (cells, systems), human anatomy, ecology, evolution, genetics, and environmental studies.

## IEB Requirements
Research tasks and practicals emphasis; no strict prereq but strong Gr9 Science advised. SBA 25% (investigations, assignments); exams 75% (two papers: knowledge/application). Focuses on ethical issues and scientific method.

## CAPS Comparison
Shared topics (Gr10 diversity, Gr11 systems, Gr12 genetics/evolution); IEB adds deeper critical analysis and links to current events like biodiversity conservation.

## University Notes
Key for health/biological sciences; min 5 (60-69%) (e.g., UP BSc Biological: 5+, SU BSc: 50% Physical/Life Sciences, UCT BSc: 70%, Wits BSc: 5+). Complements Physical Sciences for med-related paths.
