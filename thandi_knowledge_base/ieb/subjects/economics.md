## Overview
Microeconomics (markets, production), macroeconomics (growth, inflation), and contemporary issues (globalization, poverty).

## IEB Requirements
No prereq; SBA 25% (essays, graphs); exams 75% (two papers). Analytical graphs emphasized.

## CAPS Comparison
Same framework (Gr10 micro, Gr11 macro, Gr12 dynamics); IEB links to current events.

## University Notes
For econ degrees; min 5 (60-69%) (e.g., UP BEcon: 5+, UCT: 70%, Wits: 5+).
