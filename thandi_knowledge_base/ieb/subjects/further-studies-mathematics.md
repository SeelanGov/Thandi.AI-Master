## Overview
Advanced topics: calculus (limits, series), matrices, statistics (distributions), and elective modules (finance/geometry).

## IEB Requirements
Elective from Gr10-12; min 80% in core Maths. SBA 20% (tasks); exams 80% (two papers). For gifted learners.

## CAPS Comparison
Not standard in CAPS; IEB exclusive for depth beyond NSC.

## University Notes
Bonus for STEM; adds points (e.g., Wits +2, UCT +10-20 FPS, UP recognizes for advanced placement).
