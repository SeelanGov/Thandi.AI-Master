## Overview
Studies physical (climate, geomorphology) and human geography (settlements, economics, development), GIS, and environmental issues.

## IEB Requirements
No prereq; SBA 25% (research, fieldwork); exams 75% (theory, mapwork). Includes practical GIS applications.

## CAPS Comparison
Identical content (Gr10 atmosphere, Gr11 geomorph, Gr12 resources); IEB adds more sustainability focus.

## University Notes
Key for environmental/geosci; min 4 (50-59%) (e.g., UP BSc Geo: 5+, SU: 50%, UCT: 60%, Wits: 4+).
