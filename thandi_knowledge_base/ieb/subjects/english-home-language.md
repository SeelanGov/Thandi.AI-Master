## Overview
Develops language skills: comprehension, literature (novels, drama, poetry), writing (essays, transactions), orals, and visual literacy.

## IEB Requirements
Four texts per year; SBA 25% (orals, writing portfolios); exams 75% (language, literature, summary). Min 40% pass; emphasizes analytical essays and creative expression.

## CAPS Comparison
Similar structure and texts; IEB exams more interpretive with focus on critical thinking and personal response.

## University Notes
Universal requirement; min 4-5 (50-69%) (e.g., UP all programs: 4+, SU: 50-60% HL, UCT: 50%+, Wits: 5+). Higher for humanities/law (e.g., Wits LLB: 5+).
