## Overview
Covers financial accounting (statements, ratios), managerial accounting (budgets, ethics), and auditing principles.

## IEB Requirements
Min 60% in Grade 9 EMS/Math; SBA 25% (tests, projects); exams 75% (one paper). Emphasizes ethical practices.

## CAPS Comparison
Same progression (Gr10 basics, Gr11 companies, Gr12 analysis); IEB includes more case studies.

## University Notes
Required for commerce; min 5 (60-69%) (e.g., Wits BAcc: 5+, UP BCom: 5+, SU: 60%, UCT: 70%).
