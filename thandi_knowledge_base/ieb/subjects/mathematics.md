## Overview
Covers algebra (equations, inequalities, functions), calculus (differentiation, integration in Gr12), geometry (Euclidean, coordinate, trigonometry), statistics (probability, data handling), and financial mathematics.

## IEB Requirements
Problem-solving and application focus; minimum 60% in Grade 9 Mathematics recommended for continuation. SBA 25% (tasks, tests, assignments); exams 75% (two papers: algebra/trig, geometry/calc/stats). Emphasizes real-world contexts and proof-based questions.

## CAPS Comparison
Same core topics and progression (Gr10 basics, Gr11 advanced, Gr12 integration); IEB assessments more analytical and less rote, with greater emphasis on applied problems and critical reasoning.

## University Notes
Required for STEM; min 5 (60-69%) for most (e.g., UP BSc Maths: 6 (70-79%), SU BEng: 70%, UCT BSc: 70-80%, Wits BSc: 6+). Advanced Programme Mathematics provides APS bonus points at universities like Wits (+2 for 90-100%) and counts toward university admission calculations.
