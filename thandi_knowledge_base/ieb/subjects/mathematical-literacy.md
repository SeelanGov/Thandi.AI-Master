## Overview
Focuses on practical mathematics: finance (budgets, interest), data handling (stats, probability), measurements (scale, maps), and patterns/relationships in everyday contexts.

## IEB Requirements
Alternative to Mathematics; no strict prereq but suits non-STEM paths. SBA 25% (investigations, assignments); exams 75% (two papers: basic skills, applications). Emphasizes real-life scenarios.

## CAPS Comparison
Identical topics (Gr10 basics, Gr11 finance/data, Gr12 advanced apps); IEB assessments more contextual and problem-based.

## University Notes
Accepted for non-quantitative programs; min 3-4 (40-59%) (e.g., UP BA: 4+, SU Humanities: 40%, UCT/Wits: limits STEM access, prefer Maths).
