## Overview
Integrates Physics (mechanics, waves, electricity, electromagnetism) and Chemistry (matter, reactions, stoichiometry, organic chemistry, electrochemistry).

## IEB Requirements
Practical focus with experiments 15% of SBA; min 65% in Grade 9 Maths/Science prerequisite. SBA 25% (practicals, tests); exams 75% (Physics paper, Chemistry paper). Stresses inquiry-based learning and data analysis.

## CAPS Comparison
Identical content outline (Gr10 foundations, Gr11 depth, Gr12 applications); IEB includes more experimental rigor and critical evaluation in assessments.

## University Notes
Essential for engineering/sciences; min 5 (60-69%) (e.g., UP BSc Physics: 5+, SU BSc: 50% Physical/Life Sciences, UCT BSc: 70%, Wits BSc: 5-6+). Often paired with Maths 6+.
