## Overview
Covers self-development, social/environmental responsibility, health/wellness, careers, and physical education.

## IEB Requirements
Compulsory; portfolio-based only (no final exam). SBA 100% (projects, tasks, PET); min 40% for certification, 50% for university entry. Includes community service.

## CAPS Comparison
Same structure and topics; IEB integrates more ethical discussions and personal reflection.

## University Notes
Excluded from APS/FPS calculations; required pass (e.g., all unis: min 40-50%, but not scored in admissions).
