## Overview
Advanced Programme Mathematics is an enhanced IEB mathematics course that covers the standard Mathematics curriculum plus additional advanced topics. It demonstrates exceptional mathematical ability and provides significant advantages for university applications.

## Advanced Programme Benefits
Advanced Programme Mathematics offers substantial university admission benefits:
- Bonus points at universities like Wits (+2 for 90-100% achievement)
- Demonstrates advanced mathematical competency to admissions committees
- Preferred for competitive STEM programs (engineering, medicine, actuarial science)
- Shows readiness for university-level mathematics

## IEB Requirements
Requires strong performance in Grade 9 Mathematics (minimum 70% recommended). Assessment includes standard Mathematics content plus advanced problem-solving, proof techniques, and mathematical modeling. SBA 25%, final exam 75%.

## University Recognition
All major South African universities recognize Advanced Programme Mathematics:
- Wits: Includes AP Maths in APS calculation with bonus points
- UCT: Excludes from FPS but recognizes academic rigor
- UP: Counts as equivalent to standard Mathematics
- SU: Recognized for program-specific requirements
- UJ: Accepted for all mathematics prerequisites

## Career Advantages
Advanced Programme Mathematics opens doors to:
- Engineering programs (all disciplines)
- Medicine and health sciences
- Actuarial science and statistics
- Computer science and data science
- Mathematical sciences and research
- Quantitative finance and economics

## Comparison to Standard Mathematics
Covers all standard Mathematics topics plus advanced applications, mathematical modeling, and proof techniques. Demonstrates higher-order thinking and problem-solving abilities valued by universities and employers.
