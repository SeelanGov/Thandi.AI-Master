## Overview
Explores South African and global history: transformations, independence movements, civil rights, globalization, and source analysis.

## IEB Requirements
No prereq; SBA 25% (research essays, source tasks); exams 75% (two papers: source-based, extended writing). Focuses on critical interpretation.

## CAPS Comparison
Shared themes (Gr10 heritage, Gr11 communism/democracy, Gr12 apartheid); IEB emphasizes historiographical debates.

## University Notes
Useful for humanities/law; min 4 (50-59%) (e.g., Wits BA: 4+, UP History: 5+, SU: 50%, UCT: 60% for related).
