## Overview
Builds proficiency in grammar, vocabulary, literature (short stories, poems), comprehension, writing, and oral communication.

## IEB Requirements
Grammar and lit focus; SBA 25% (orals, essays); exams 75% (language structures, literature). Min 40% pass; suitable for non-home speakers, with immigrant options.

## CAPS Comparison
Identical progression (Gr10 basics, Gr11 lit depth, Gr12 advanced); IEB assessments independent, with more emphasis on communicative competence.

## University Notes
Meets language requirement; min 4 (50-59%) (e.g., SU: 50% FAL, UP: 4+, UCT/Wits: accepted as additional but English HL preferred). Useful for multilingual programs.
