## Overview
SU recognizes IEB NSC equivalently to CAPS, using aggregate % from best 6 subjects excluding LO.

## IEB Admission Requirements
APS calculation: Aggregate % from best 6 excluding Life Orientation
AP subject policy: Included if among best; no bonuses (IEB-specific enhancement)
Minimum endorsement: Bachelor's degree pass

## Faculty Requirements

### Engineering and Built Environment
Minimum APS: 70% aggregate
Mathematics: 70% (Level 6)
Physical Sciences: 60% (Level 5)
English: 50% (Level 4)
AP Mathematics: Included for thresholds

### Commerce and Management
Minimum APS: 65-80% aggregate
Mathematics: 60-80% (Level 5-7)
English: 50-70% (Level 4-6)
AP Mathematics: Included

### Humanities and Social Sciences
Minimum APS: 60-65% aggregate
English: 50% (Level 4)
Mathematics: 50% (Level 4) or Math Lit 70% (Level 6)

## Program Examples
BEng Chemical: 70% aggregate, Math 70% (Level 6), Phys Sci 60% (Level 5), English 50% (Level 4)
BCom Management: 65% aggregate, Math 60% (Level 5), English 50% (Level 4)
BA Humanities: 63% aggregate, English 50% (Level 4), Math 50% (Level 4)
