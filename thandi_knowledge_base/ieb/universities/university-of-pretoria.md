## Overview
UP recognizes IEB NSC equivalently to CAPS, using APS on best 6 subjects excluding LO.

## IEB Admission Requirements
APS calculation: Best 6 subjects excluding Life Orientation
AP subject policy: Included as Mathematics equivalent; no bonuses (IEB-specific recognition)
Minimum endorsement: Bachelor's degree pass

## Faculty Requirements

### Engineering and Built Environment
Minimum APS: 23-35
Mathematics: Level 5-6 (60-79%)
Physical Sciences: Level 5-6 (60-79%)
English: Level 4-5 (50-69%)
AP Mathematics: Included as equivalent

### Commerce and Management
Minimum APS: 28-32
Mathematics: Level 4-5 (50-69%)
English: Level 4 (50-59%)
AP Mathematics: Included

### Humanities and Social Sciences
Minimum APS: 28-30
English: Level 4-5 (50-69%)
Mathematics: Level 4 (50-59%) if required

## Program Examples
BEng: APS 35, Math Level 6 (70-79%), Phys Sci Level 6 (70-79%), English Level 5 (60-69%)
BCom: APS 30, Math Level 5 (60-69%), English Level 4 (50-59%)
BA: APS 28, English Level 4 (50-59%), Math Level 4 (50-59%)
