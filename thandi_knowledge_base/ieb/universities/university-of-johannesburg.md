## Overview
UJ recognizes IEB NSC equivalently to CAPS, using APS on best 6 subjects excluding LO.

## IEB Admission Requirements
APS calculation: Best 6 subjects excluding Life Orientation
AP subject policy: Included if equivalent to NSC; no bonuses (IEB-specific exemption possible)
Minimum endorsement: Bachelor's degree pass

## Faculty Requirements

### Engineering and Built Environment
Minimum APS: 23-32
Mathematics: Level 5 (60-69%)
Physical Sciences: Level 5 (60-69%)
English: Level 4-5 (50-69%)
AP Mathematics: Included as equivalent; no bonuses

### Commerce and Management
Minimum APS: 26-33
Mathematics: Level 4-5 (50-69%)
English: Level 4 (50-59%)
AP Mathematics: Included; Math Lit conditional

### Humanities and Social Sciences
Minimum APS: 25-31
English: Level 4-5 (50-69%)
Mathematics: Level 3-5 (40-69%) if required

## Program Examples
BArch: APS 30, Math Level 5 (60-69%), English Level 5 (60-69%)
BCom Accounting: APS 28, Math Level 4 (50-59%), English Level 4 (50-59%)
BA Visual Art: APS 25, English Level 5 (60-69%), Math Level 3 (40-49%)
