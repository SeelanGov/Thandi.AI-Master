## Overview
UCT recognizes IEB NSC using FPS (Faculty Point Score system, which combines APS + NBT scores), excludes Advanced Programme subjects from calculation.

## IEB Admission Requirements
APS calculation: Best 6 subjects excluding Life Orientation and AP subjects (uses FPS - Faculty Point Score system)
AP subject policy: Advanced Programme subjects explicitly excluded from APS/FPS calculations; no bonus points awarded
Minimum endorsement: Bachelor's degree pass

## Faculty Requirements

### Engineering and Built Environment
Minimum FPS: 450-500
Mathematics: 75-80% (Level 6-7)
Physical Sciences: 70-75% (Level 6)
English: 50-60% (Level 4-5)
AP Mathematics: Excluded from calculation

### Commerce and Management
Minimum FPS: 435-500
Mathematics: 60-80% (Level 5-7)
English: 50-80% (Level 4-7)
AP Mathematics: Excluded

### Humanities and Social Sciences
Minimum FPS: 450
English: 50-60% (Level 4-5)
Mathematics: 60%+ if required by program

## Program Examples
BSc Computer Science: FPS 450+, Math 75% (Level 6-7), English 60% (Level 5)
BCom Accounting: FPS 480+, Math 70% (Level 6), English 60% (Level 5)
BA Psychology: FPS 450, English 60% (Level 5), Math not required

## Flagged Conflicts
Uses FPS (includes NBTs), not standard APS
AP subjects explicitly excluded (differs from Wits/UJ)
