What Are SETA Learnerships?

Learnerships combine classroom learning and work experience for 12–18 months. They’re funded by SETAs and lead to NQF qualifications plus a stipend.

Benefit: Get paid while learning skills for real jobs.

Eligibility

  • Education: Grade 10–12; some require subjects like Maths.
  • Age: Usually 18–35 for youth programs.
