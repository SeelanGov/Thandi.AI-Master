# SETA Pathways Knowledge Base

## Overview
Comprehensive database of all 21 Sector Education and Training Authorities (SETAs) in South Africa, providing learnership and occupational qualification pathways for students who want practical, industry-focused training.

## Purpose
Enable Thandi to guide students toward:
- Sector-specific skills training
- Learnerships and apprenticeships
- Occupational qualifications (NQF levels 1-8)
- Industry-recognized certifications
- Direct employment pathways

## Coverage
- **Total SETAs**: 21
- **Geographic Coverage**: National (all provinces)
- **Programme Types**: Learnerships, Apprenticeships, Occupational Qualifications
- **NQF Levels**: 1-8
- **Entry Requirements**: Grade 9 to Grade 12 depending on qualification

## Key SETA Categories

### Manufacturing & Engineering
- MERSETA (Manufacturing, Engineering)
- CHIETA (Chemical Industries)
- FP&M SETA (Fibre Processing & Manufacturing)
- FOODBEV SETA (Food & Beverages)

### Services & Business
- BANKSETA (Banking)
- FASSET (Finance & Accounting)
- INSETA (Insurance)
- Services SETA (Beauty, Hairdressing, Real Estate)
- W&RSETA (Wholesale & Retail)

### Infrastructure & Construction
- CETA (Construction)
- EWSETA (Energy & Water)
- TETA (Transport)

### Technology & Media
- MICT SETA (Media, ICT)

### Public Sector
- PSETA (Public Service)
- LGSETA (Local Government)
- SASSETA (Safety & Security)

### Specialized Sectors
- AgriSETA (Agriculture)
- CATHSSETA (Culture, Arts, Tourism, Hospitality, Sport)
- ETDP SETA (Education, Training & Development)
- HWSETA (Health & Welfare)
- MQA (Mining)

## Data Structure
Each SETA entry includes:
- Full name and abbreviation
- Location (province, municipality, town, coordinates)
- Programme types and examples
- NQF levels
- Entry requirements (minimum grade, subjects)
- Career outcomes
- Contact information
- Application links

## Use Cases
1. **Career-to-SETA Mapping**: "I want to be an electrician" → EWSETA
2. **Subject-to-SETA Matching**: "I have Maths & Science" → MERSETA, CHIETA, EWSETA
3. **Alternative Pathways**: Students who don't qualify for university
4. **Practical Skills Focus**: Students who prefer hands-on learning
5. **Industry Connections**: Direct links to employment sectors

## Integration with Thandi
- Embedded in RAG system for semantic search
- Linked to career pathways
- Connected to subject requirements
- Mapped to geographic locations
- Integrated with funding/bursary information

## Update Frequency
- Annual review (SETAs update programmes yearly)
- Contact information verified quarterly
- Application links checked before peak application periods

## Data Source
Official SETA websites and QCTO (Quality Council for Trades and Occupations) documentation, November 2025.
