## üìà Quality Assurance Framework

### Content Standards (Every Module Must Have)
1. **SA-Specific Context:** Local companies, ZAR salaries, market demand
2. **Education Pathways:** University requirements, TVET options, certifications
3. **Financial Reality:** Entry/mid/senior salaries, job security, growth outlook
4. **Funding Options:** NSFAS eligibility, bursaries, alternative funding
5. **Action Steps:** Concrete next steps for Grade 11/12 students

### Expert Validation Network
**Establish partnerships with:**

### Continuous Testing Protocol
**Weekly validation:**
- Run expanded test suite (50+ questions covering all career areas)
- Target: 80% pass rate on core careers, 70% on emerging careers
- Expert review of 5 random modules per week
- Student feedback integration from alpha/beta testing
