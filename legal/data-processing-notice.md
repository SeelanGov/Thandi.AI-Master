## 4. AUTOMATED DECISION-MAKING & PROFILING

### AI Processing Activities:

**We use machine learning algorithms to:**
1. **Analyze** academic performance patterns
2. **Predict** university admission likelihood (probability scores)
3. **Match** students to career pathways based on multi-variable analysis
4. **Recommend** bursaries based on eligibility criteria
5. **Generate** personalized reports with explanations

### Logic Involved:
- **Training data:** Historical admission data (2020-2024), career outcome surveys
- **Variables:** Subject marks, grade trends, APS scores, institutional admission stats
- **Methodology:** Supervised learning, collaborative filtering, regression analysis
- **Confidence levels:** Expressed as percentages (e.g., "78% match") - not certainties

### Significance of Decisions:

**Our AI does NOT make binding decisions.** It provides **recommendations only.** Final decisions are made by:
- Students and parents
- School counselors
- University admissions committees
- Bursary selection panels

### Your Rights:
- **Request explanation:** Understand why a recommendation was made
- **Request re-evaluation:** With additional information provided
- **Object to automated processing:** Request human review (contact hello@thandi.online)
- **Contest decisions:** Provide mitigating circumstances not captured by AI

**To exercise rights:** Email hello@thandi.online with subject "AI Decision Review"
