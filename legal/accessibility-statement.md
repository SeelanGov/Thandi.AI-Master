## 1. OUR COMMITMENT

Thandi AI (PTY) LTD is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all South African students, including those with disabilities. We believe every student deserves equal access to quality career guidance, regardless of their abilities.

**Core Principles:**
- **Ubuntu-based:** Accessibility is not a feature, it's our foundation
- **Nothing about us without us:** Students with disabilities involved in design
- **Progressive enhancement:** Works for everyone, works better for modern browsers
- **Bandwidth-aware:** Accessible on low-speed connections and older devices

**Target Compliance:** Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA

## 2. CURRENT ACCESSIBILITY STATUS (BETA)

### 2.1 Known Limitations (Beta Phase)

**Visual Accessibility:**
- ⚠️ **Color contrast:** Some charts/graphs do not meet 4.5:1 ratio (being fixed)
- ⚠️ **Alt text:** Some decorative images missing alt attributes
- ⚠️ **Screen reader:** Complex recommendation tables need better ARIA labels

**Motor Accessibility:**
- ⚠️ **Keyboard navigation:** Some interactive elements not fully keyboard-accessible
- ⚠️ **Focus indicators:** Visible focus needs improvement on some buttons

**Cognitive Accessibility:**
- ⚠️ **Plain language:** Some technical terms need simpler explanations
- ⚠️ **Error messages:** Some form errors not clearly explained

**Auditory Accessibility:**
- ✅ **No audio content:** No videos or audio requiring captions (yet)

**Seizure & Vestibular:**
- ✅ **No flashing content:** No elements flashing more than 3 times per second

### 2.2 Assistive Technology Compatibility

**Screen Readers:**
- ⚠️ **NVDA (Windows):** Partially compatible (tested with 2025.1)
- ⚠️ **JAWS (Windows):** Partially compatible (tested with 2025)
- ⚠️ **VoiceOver (iOS/macOS):** Partially compatible (tested with iOS 17)
- ✖️ **TalkBack (Android):** Not yet tested (planned for January 2026)

**Speech Recognition:**
- ⚠️ **Dragon NaturallySpeaking:** Basic commands work, complex navigation pending
- ✅ **Windows Speech Recognition:** Core functionality works

**Alternative Input:**
- ⚠️ **Keyboard-only:** 80% functional (known gaps in assessment wizard)
- ⚠️ **Switch access:** Not yet supported (planned for Q1 2026)
- ✖️ **Sip-and-puff:** Not yet supported

**Magnification:**
- ✅ **Browser zoom:** Works up to 200% zoom
- ⚠️ **Text spacing:** Some layout breaks at custom text spacing (being fixed)

## 3. ACCESSIBLE FEATURES (CURRENT)

### 3.1 Visual Features
- ✅ **Responsive design:** Works on mobile, tablet, desktop
- ✅ **Scalable text:** Text can be resized without breaking layout (most pages)
- ✅ **Clear headings:** Proper heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3)
- ✅ **Link purpose:** Descriptive link text (not "click here")
- ✅ **No content-only-by-color:** Information not conveyed by color alone (mostly)

### 3.2 Motor Features
- ✅ **Large click targets:** Buttons minimum 44x44px
- ✅ **No time limits:** Students can take assessment at their own pace
- ✅ **Simple navigation:** Consistent navigation menu across pages
- ⚠️ **Skip links:** Present but not yet functional on all pages

### 3.3 Cognitive Features
- ✅ **Plain language:** Average readability grade 8-9 level
- ✅ **Logical flow:** Assessment follows clear step-by-step process
- ✅ **Error prevention:** Form validation before submission
- ✅ **Progress indicators:** Shows where you are in the process
- ✅ **Consistent layout:** Same structure across all pages

### 3.4 Technical Features
- ✅ **Valid HTML:** Semantic HTML5 structure
- ✅ **ARIA landmarks:** Some implemented (banner, main, navigation)
- ✅ **Form labels:** All inputs have associated labels
- ⚠️ **ARIA live regions:** Not yet implemented for dynamic content
