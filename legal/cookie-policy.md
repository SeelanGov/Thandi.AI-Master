## WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to improve your experience. We use cookies in compliance with POPIA and the Electronic Communications and Transactions Act.

## TYPES OF COOKIES WE USE

### 1. ESSENTIAL COOKIES

**Required for basic functionality:**
- Session management (keeping you logged in)
- Assessment progress tracking
- Security tokens (reCAPTCHA)
- Beta feature access control

**You cannot opt out** of essential cookies.

### 2. FUNCTIONALITY COOKIES

**Improve user experience:**
- Language preference (English, isiZulu, Afrikaans)
- Accessibility settings
- Beta feedback forms

These enhance functionality but are not strictly necessary.

### 3. ANALYTICS COOKIES

**Help us improve (critical for beta):**
- **Google Analytics 4** (anonymized IP, no individual tracking)
- **Mixpanel** (user journey analysis for bug detection)
- **Hotjar** (session recordings - fully anonymized)

**Data processing:** All analytics data is anonymized and cannot be traced to individual students.

### 4. MARKETING COOKIES

**Currently NONE during beta phase.** We do not display ads or share data for marketing.

## SPECIFIC COOKIES USED

| Cookie Name | Type | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|------|---------|----------|
| `thandi_session` | Essential | Session management | 2 hours |
| `thandi_preferences` | Functional | User settings | 1 year |
| `thandi_beta_consent` | Essential | Beta participation consent | Session |
| `_ga` | Analytics | Google Analytics | 2 years |
| `_gid` | Analytics | Google Analytics | 24 hours |
| `_gat` | Analytics | Rate limiting | 1 minute |

## THIRD-PARTY COOKIES

- **Google:** Analytics, reCAPTCHA (security)
- **Vercel:** Infrastructure monitoring and performance analytics (hosting provider)
- **GoDaddy:** Domain registration services (no cookies placed on user devices)

**All third-party providers are POPIA/GDPR compliant.** Data is processed under Standard Contractual Clauses.

## MANAGING COOKIES

### Browser Settings:
- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy > Cookies
- **Firefox:** Options > Privacy > Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy

### Our Cookie Banner:
Click "Cookie Settings" in footer to:
- View all active cookies
- Opt out of non-essential cookies
- Clear cookie preferences

## CONSENT DURATION

Your cookie consent is valid for 12 months, after which we will re-prompt. You can change preferences anytime via the footer link.

## POPIA COMPLIANCE

- Cookie banner appears on first visit (explicit opt-in)
- Granular consent options provided (separate toggles)
- Easy withdrawal of consent (one-click reset)
- No non-essential cookie set before consent
- Consent records maintained for 5 years (auditable)

## CROSS-BORDER COOKIES

Some analytics cookies involve EU/US servers. All transfers comply with POPIA Section 72 through Standard Contractual Clauses and vendor data processing agreements.

## STUDENT PRIVACY PROTECTION

- **Under 18:** Parental consent required for non-essential cookies
- **School devices:** We detect education networks and minimize tracking
- **Anonymization:** Student IDs are hashed before analytics processing
- **No profiling:** We do not create behavioral profiles for advertising
