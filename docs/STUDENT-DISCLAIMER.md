## How to Use THANDI.AI

### 1. Ask Clear Questions
```
Good: "What do I need to become a software engineer?"
Good: "I don't have matric, what can I do?"
Good: "Tell me about careers that earn good money quickly"
```

### 2. Be Honest About Your Situation
- If you don't have matric, say so
- If you hate a subject, mention it
- If you have financial constraints, include that

### 3. Ask Follow-Up Questions
Don't stop at the first answer! Ask:
- "Tell me more about..."
- "What are the alternatives?"
- "How long does this take?"
- "What does this career pay?"

### 4. Talk to Your Teacher
**Always discuss THANDI.AI's suggestions with your teacher before making big decisions.**
